The Extraordinary Macaw

  • Members of the Parrot family
  • Built to fly through the trees in a forest
  • Coloring suited for life in the rainforests of Central and Southern Americas
  • Intelligent and social
  • Incredible life-span - If considering a macaw as a pet keep in mind they may out-live you
  • Social with a great memory - This means a macaw who loses its family can quickly become depressed
  • Intelligent and capable - Macaws can learn an incredible amount including diffrentiating colors and shapes

For more information please visit The San Diego Zoo's page on Macaw's
